Why do I need a financial advisor?

Financial independence and security is a goal we all share. A financial advisor can help you identify and quantify your financial goals, develop a plan to achieve these objectives, and consult with you along along the way to insure that you are always driving towards success.

Why choose FFA?

Forbes Financial Advocates has been helping individuals and families achieve their financial goals for over 15 years. We offer a full range of investment alternatives by partnering with most of the leading financial services providers in the world. The trust and confidence we earn with our clients by providing recommendations that are always in the client’s best financial interest set us apart.

Products and Services

Investment & Allocation Strategies

Develop an asset allocation strategy that will optimize your portfolio’s ability to meet your near and longer term goals.

Retirement Income & Wealth Security

Ensure that you will be able to maintain your current standard of living, as well as address potential increases in health care expenditures, and purse leisure & other activities of interest in your retirement.

Legacy/estate & Life Events Planning

Plan for life’s inevitable financial demands and provide a remembrance of your achievements for your family.

Asset Protection

Ensure that your nest egg will have protection from inevitable downturns in the market with a guaranteed steady level of income in all conditions.

College Saving Plans

Lighten the burden of ever increasing college tuition and related expenses for your children & grandchildren by employing tax advantaged savings plans.

Pricing

There is never a charge for your initial consultation. If there is mutual agreement to proceed, we offer to prepare an investment proposal for your consideration. All potential future fees are kept at a reasonable level and will be clearly explained before proceeding.
